About me

I am a Space Systems Engineer currently working as a Research Engineer at the National University of Singapore (NUS) on ionospheric characterization and space weather. My work focuses on multi-receiver GNSS data analysis, equatorial plasma bubble (EPB) detection and velocity estimation using machine learning and signal processing methods. I am skilled in satcom system design, deep learning signal analysis, Python/MATLAB communications-chain development and STK mission planning. I am interested in R&D roles in NewSpace, navigation, and deep tech.
