Publications & Conferences

All submissions listed below are accepted. EuCAP 2026 has already been presented, while the other conferences are upcoming in 2026.

  • EuCAP 2026 (Dublin, Ireland) - Leray et al.: Automatic Detection of Equatorial Plasma Bubbles Using Rate of TEC Index from SiReNT GNSS Data
    Presented Accepted conference paper (2026).
    Neyman-Pearson detector with deep learning signal decomposition (sum of Gaussians), and EPB clustering by IoU over Gaussian envelopes.
  • AP/S-URSI 2026 (Detroit, USA) - Leray et al.: Estimation of Equatorial Plasma Bubble Drift Velocities Using Dynamic Time Warping
    Upcoming Accepted conference paper (2026).
    Constrained inter-receiver DTW alignment with drift velocity estimation using a planar wavefront model. Validation performed on a synthetic EPB simulator.
  • URSI-GASS 2026 (Krakow, Poland) - Leray et al.: Equatorial Plasma Bubble Velocity Estimation over Southeast Asia
    Upcoming Accepted conference paper (2026).
    DTW-based velocity estimation framework on SiReNT data with spatial interpolation.
  • APCAP 2026 (Chengdu, China) - Leray et al.: Occurrence and Characteristics of Post-Sunset Equatorial Plasma Bubbles over Singapore
    Upcoming Accepted conference paper (2026).
    Two-year analysis of post-sunset EPB characteristics based on ROTI detection results.
